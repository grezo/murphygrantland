In April 1997, J. R. Murphy and John M. Grantland founded the firm along with the help of a paralegal and receptionist. With the mission of providing high-quality legal services with uncompromising integrity and uncommon value, Murphy & Grantland, P.A. flourished and more than doubled its client base and workload.

Over the next decade, Murphy & Grantland, P.A. expanded to include over a dozen attorneys, and a full-fledged legal and office support staff. After outgrowing three different office locations, Murphy & Grantland, P.A. built its present location at 4406-B Forest Drive in Columbia in 2005.
